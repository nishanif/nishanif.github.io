Leave Projection Calculator

Australian NES: Annual leave (4 weeks / 7.6923%) and Personal leave (10 days / 1/26 of ordinary hours).

Works for full-time & part-time

Accrual rates: Annual leave 0.076923 × ordinary hours; Personal leave 1/26 of ordinary hours. Unpaid leave reduces accruals.
